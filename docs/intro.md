# Introduction

Cyfamod is a modern **open-source school management system** designed to streamline school operations, enhance learning experiences, and foster better communication between all stakeholders â€” teachers, students, parents, and administrators.

Whether you're managing students, tracking fees, scheduling exams, or communicating with parents, Cyfamod offers a unified solution built with scalability and ease of use in mind.

## Key Features

- **Multi-Tenant Architecture** - Manage multiple schools or branches with complete data isolation
- **Student & Staff Management** - Enrollment, attendance, performance tracking
- **Fee Management** - Payment tracking, receipts, installment plans, financial reports
- **Results & Grading** - Automated grading, report cards, academic analytics
- **Computer-Based Testing (CBT)** - Online exams with automated grading and instant results
- **Role-Based Access Control** - Granular permissions for admins, teachers, students, and parents
- **Parent Portal** - Real-time access to student progress, attendance, and communication
- **Attendance Tracking** - Automated attendance with notifications and reports
- **Timetable Management** - Class scheduling and resource allocation
- **Communication Tools** - Announcements, notifications, and messaging
