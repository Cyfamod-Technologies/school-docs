# Student Guide

Learn how to use the School Management System as a student.

## Logging In

1. Navigate to the school portal URL
2. Click **"Student Login"**
3. Enter your email and password
4. Click **"Sign In"**

## Viewing Your Dashboard

After logging in, you'll see:

- **Classes** - Your enrolled courses
- **Assignments** - Pending and completed work
- **Grades** - Your academic performance
- **Attendance** - Your attendance record
- **Announcements** - Important updates

## Submitting Assignments

1. Go to **"Assignments"**
2. Click on an assignment
3. Upload your work
4. Click **"Submit"**
